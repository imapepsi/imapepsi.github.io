About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm a PhD student studying Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), with a focus on how autistic individuals engage with social media. My research explores ways to design more inclusive, supportive online spaces that empower neurodiverse users to connect, express themselves, and thrive in digital environments.

When I'm not doing research, you can find me exploring new technologies, developing games, or spending time with my family. I believe in the power of technology to make a positive impact on people's lives.
