About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm a PhD student studying Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), with a focus on how autistic individuals engage with social media. My research explores ways to educate neurodiverse users in safe privacy practices so they can thrive in digital environments.

When I'm not doing research, you can find me exploring new technologies, developing games, or spending time with my family.
